1. Title: Plant-Based Food Alternatives

2. The purpose of this project is to give insight into how people can easily substitute animal products in their diet to plant-based alternatives. I would like to disprove the misconception that vegetarian and vegan diets are expensive, restrictive, or bland. This will be done by displaying alternatives for certain nutrition needs that are widely accessable and affordable.
